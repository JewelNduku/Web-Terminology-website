Intranet

An Intranet is a private network that uses Internet protocols and technologies to share information and resources within an organization. Examples: Corporate networks, internal websites Functions: Facilitates communication and collaboration within an organization Provides secure access to internal resources

Advantages: Improved communication, enhanced collaboration, centralized information management

Disadvantages: Limited access for external users, potential security risks if not properly managed

Example: A company may have an intranet that allows employees to access company news, internal documents, and collaboration tools.
