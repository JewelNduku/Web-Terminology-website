Transmission Control Protocol

This is the foundation of internet communication.TCP (Transmission Control Protocol),Breaks data into packets and ensures packets arrive correctly and in order.

IP (Internet Protocol)Handles addressing (where data goes),Like a “delivery address system”.

Simple idea:TCP = makes sure your message is complete,IP = sends it to the right place.

Advantages: Reliable, connection-oriented, handles congestion control.

Disadvantages: Can be slower than UDP (User Datagram Protocol) due to error-checking and connection setup.

Example: When you send a WhatsApp message → TCP/IP ensures it reaches your friend correctly.
